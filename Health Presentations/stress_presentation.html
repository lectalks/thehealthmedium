Kahoot! : What Do You Know About Stress?

Purpose

This presentation is to raise awareness in reducing stress among teenagers and educate students about its signs, symptoms, and treatment.

Hangman:

Get into groups of four. You will be playing hangman. Have each group guess a letter one at a time. The answers are some of the signs of depression. Each group will go in order and guess repeatedly until one can say the correct answer.

Play Hangman!

Note: There are 5 words for this hangman, so once your class has passed 5 words, end the game. Online hangman unfortuanely does not end itself when all words are used.

Extra document: Answers To The Hangman Game

Solutions to The Hangman Activity

Talk:

Talk to your group and figure out some possible symptoms of depression. Do this for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes, your instructor will read to you what is correct.

Answer:

The symptoms are the same as the signs

Treatment:

Talk to friends, family, teachers, and counselors. Let them know what is going on in your life. They will do their best to help you.

Also, try consulting staff at the Wellness Center. They are always willing to give you a helping hand.

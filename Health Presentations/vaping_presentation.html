Kahoot! : What Do You Know About Vaping?

Purpose

This presentation is to raise awareness in stopping teenage vaping by informing students about the signs, symptoms, and treatments.

Pictionary: Round 1

Get into groups of 4. Grab a piece of paper and have a scribe who will record the answers for the team. Then, determine who will be the reporter. You will be shown a series of slides that have pictures describing the signs of vaping. When your group is shown the first picture, talk to one another and have the scribe write down the group’s guess. There will be a total of 6 photos for this game, so number your answers accordingly. Once the instructor sees that everybody is done, he or she will move on to the next slide for the next picture. Students will repeat the process. After students guess on the last picture, the instructor will reveal the answers.

Guess the major signs of teenage vaping addiction with a picture.

ANSWERS:

  1. Chronic cough (hacking cough)
  2. Blisters on fingertips
  3. Feeling Extremely Thirsty
  4. Poor academic performance
  5. Blood Pressure/Irregular Heartbeat
  6. Nose Bleeding

Challenging Activity!

What side effects do you think vaping has? Discuss with your group. Come up with 3 questions to ask when determining whether someone has vaping addiction. After 5 minutes, walk around and share your thoughts with at least 2 other groups. Do this for 5 minutes. Then, sit back down. Afterwards, the instructor will show you the answers on the next slide. In order to get the right answers, think critically.

Hints for the Answers:

Does a vaping addict like to taste a variety of flavors?

Does this person like to "step outside" often? If so, to do what exactly?

Is shortage of breath a side effect?

ANSWERS:

  • Is this person suddenly in need to try out a variety of flavors?
  • Does this person have to “step outside” often – but really to vape?
  • Did this person recently have a shortage of breath?

Treatment:

To take the first steps in vaping withdrawal, try visiting:
